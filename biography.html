Biography

Dr. Dikko Umar Radda was born on 10 September 1969 in Dutsin-Ma, Katsina State. He attended Radda Primary School (1974–1980) and Zaria Teachers College (1980–1985). He earned his NCE at Kafanchan College of Education (1990), B.Tech in Agricultural Economics at ATBU Bauchi (1996), M.Sc. at ABU Zaria, and later a Ph.D. in Agricultural Extension and Rural Sociology.

His career began as a classroom teacher, before entering politics where he served as APC Welfare Secretary (2014), Chief of Staff to Governor Aminu Bello Masari (2015), Director-General of SMEDAN (2016), and finally became Governor of Katsina State in May 2023.
